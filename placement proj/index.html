Our Training and Placement Cell acts as a crucial bridge between our students and the corporate world, ensuring a smooth transition from academic life to professional careers. Training Methodology

1st Year: Communication and Personality Development Programs

We focus on enhancing students' communication skills and building their confidence through interactive sessions, workshops, and activities that foster personal growth.

2nd Year: Knowledge and Skill Development Programs

The training includes enhancing aptitude, logical reasoning skills, and building foundational knowledge in programming languages. These programs aim to develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

3rd Year: Specialization Development Programs

Students receive specialized training tailored to their chosen fields, preparing them for specific industry roles.

4th Year: Industry-Oriented Development Programs

Training focuses on aligning students with current industry needs and practices. These programs provide practical insights and hands-on experience to ensure readiness for the professional world.
